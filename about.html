About Campbell's

Two working mechanics, one shared standard. Get the job done right the first time, where the vehicle actually is.

Family Run
The Story

Built in the field. Run from home.

Mike Campbell spent eight years turning wrenches in the Texas oilfields before taking his trade general. Ty Shelton came up working on anything with an engine, from daily drivers to ag equipment. Between them they've seen most of what can go wrong with a vehicle, and a fair bit of what shouldn't.

They started Campbell's because they were tired of watching customers lose half a day to a tow and a waiting room. The work is the same. The location shouldn't have to be.

How We Operate

The standard we hold.

01

Diagnose, Don't Guess

Parts swapping is expensive and rarely fixes the actual problem. We do the diagnostic work first so you only pay for what your vehicle actually needs.

02

Show Up On Time

If we say 9 AM, we mean 9 AM. Your time is worth more than a four-hour service window. We schedule realistically and we honor it.

03

Quote It Straight

One price, no surprise add-ons. If we find something else while we're there, you get a phone call before the wrench moves. Your call, not ours.

04

Work On Anything

Cars, trucks, diesels, tractors, trailers, boats, skid steers, lifts. If it has a motor, we want to look at it. Versatility is a service.

05

Local & Accountable

We live and work here. You'll see us at the same gas stations, hardware stores, and BBQ joints. That keeps us honest in a way a corporate shop can't be.

06

Available When You Are

Mornings, evenings, weekends, after-hours. We answer the phone. We respond to texts. Your work week shouldn't stop for a repair.
