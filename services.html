What We Do

Services

If it has wheels or a motor, we probably work on it. Here's the full list. Don't see what you need? Call. We almost certainly handle it.

Cars & Light Trucks

Auto service & repair.

The everyday stuff, done right the first time. Mobile or in-shop, your call.

Oil Changes

Conventional, synthetic blend, full synthetic. Filter, fluids checked, tires checked. We'll come to your driveway and have it done before you finish coffee.

Brake Replacements

Pads, rotors, calipers, lines, master cylinders. Soft pedal, grinding, pulling, vibration. We diagnose the cause, not just throw parts at it.

Tune-Ups

Plugs, coils, wires, filters, belts. Restore lost power, fix rough idle, knock that check engine light off the dash.

Suspension & Shocks

Front and rear shocks, struts, control arms, bushings, sway bar links. If it bounces, leans, or clunks, we'll find it.

A/C Service

Recharge, leak diagnosis, compressor replacement, condenser work. Texas heat is no joke. We'll get cold air back.

Battery & Electrical

Battery replacement, alternator and starter testing, parasitic draws, wiring repair. No-start diagnosis without a guess-and-buy approach.

Diesel & Heavy Duty

Diesel specialists.

From a 7.3 Powerstroke that won't fire to a Mahindra tractor that's been sitting for two years. Diesel work is in our DNA.

Glow Plugs & Injectors

Hard cold starts, miss codes, rough idle. We pull, test, and replace plugs and injectors. Get the truck back in service the same day.

Fuel System Service

Tank cleaning, fuel hoses, filters, hard line replacement, injector cleaning. The full fuel system reset for a no-start diesel.

In-Depth Diagnostics

Live data, freeze frame, scope work. The tough drivability issues other shops want to throw a parts cannon at, we actually solve.

Field Service

Heavy equipment & farm.

Skid steers, backhoes, scissor lifts, tractors. The project doesn't stop because a piece of equipment laid down. We bring the tools to the field.

Skid Steers & Loaders

Hydraulics, drive motors, electrical, engine work. Bobcat, John Deere, Case, Cat. Field-serviceable repairs without a shop trip.

Tractors

Mahindra, John Deere, Kubota, New Holland. Fuel systems, hydraulics, PTOs, electrical. We come to the barn.

Lifts & Aerials

Scissor lifts, boom lifts, articulating lifts. Hydraulic, electrical, and mechanical service so the crew doesn't lose a day.

Towing Season

Trailers, boats, & RVs.

Bearings, hubs, brakes, axles, lights. Get the rig inspected before launch day or hauling season.

Bearings & Hubs

Inspect, repack, or replace. Boat trailers, goosenecks, utility trailers. We've done 32 footers in the dirt and 14 footers in driveways.

Trailer Brakes

Electric brake systems, drum brakes, controllers. Get the trailer stopping the way it should before you load it down.

Axles & Suspension

Spring replacements, equalizers, shackles, axle service. Heavy haul trailers, boat trailers, dump trailers, RVs.

How We Work

The shop comes to you.

Most jobs we can do on-site, on your driveway, in your work parking lot, or out at the job site. For bigger work or specialized tools we bring it back to the shop. Either way, you get one quote and one timeline.

Call or text any time. We work mornings, evenings, weekends, and the in-between. Your work week shouldn't stop because of a check engine light.
