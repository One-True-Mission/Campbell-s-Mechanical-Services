The Team
Built On The Job

Two mechanics. Seventeen years. One promise.

Campbell's was started by Mike Campbell and Ty Shelton, two working mechanics who got tired of watching customers lose a whole day waiting on a shop. Mike spent eight years in the oilfields before going general. Ty came up turning wrenches on anything with an engine.

Now they bring the shop to your driveway, your job site, or wherever your truck quit on you. Same diagnostics, same repairs, none of the towing.

What We Work On

Versatile by design.

From an oil change in the driveway to a full fuel system service on a tractor, we handle the whole spread. If it has wheels or a motor, give us a call.

Auto Service & Repair

Oil changes, brakes, tune-ups, suspension, A/C work. Cars, light trucks, SUVs. Mobile or in-shop.

In-Depth Diagnostics

Code reads, drivability issues, intermittent faults. We don't guess and swap parts. We find the actual problem.

Diesel & Heavy Duty

Glow plugs, fuel system service, injectors, hard lines. From 7.3 Powerstrokes to Mahindra tractors.

Trailers & Boats

Bearings, hubs, brakes, axle work. Goosenecks, boat trailers, utility trailers. Get the rig ready before launch day.

Heavy Equipment

Skid steers, backhoes, scissor lifts, tractors. Field service so the project keeps moving.
